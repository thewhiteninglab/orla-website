FAQ

Got questions?

Unlike hydrogen peroxide-based whiteners, our PAP+ formula is clinically proven to whiten without causing sensitivity. We've also added Hydroxyapatite to actively strengthen and remineralize enamel during treatment. The vast majority of users — including those with previously sensitive teeth — report zero discomfort.
Most users notice a difference within the first 2–3 uses. For full results (up to 8+ shades whiter), complete the full 7-day treatment. Visible whitening is backed by clinical testing on real users — not staged photography.
Our formula is designed for natural tooth enamel and will not damage dental work. However, it will not whiten existing crowns, veneers, or composite bonding — only natural teeth. We recommend consulting your dentist if you have significant dental restorations.
Results typically last 4–6 months with the full kit. To maximize longevity, we recommend using the Touch-Up Pen 2–3x per week and our Brightening Toothpaste daily. Avoid dark-staining foods and beverages in the first 48 hours after treatment.
We offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your results within 30 days of purchase, contact our support team for a full refund — no need to return the product.
